Examples Of Healthy Food

Salad

Salad is an important source of daily vegetable fiber, which is found in vegetables such as lettuce, spinach, collard greens, and kale.

whole grains

Eating foods that contain whole grains or fruits and vegetables can also reduce the risk of developing gestational diabetes.

Fruits

Fruits contain vitamins and minerals that protect the body from many diseases as well as maintain the beauty of hair and the freshness of the skin.
